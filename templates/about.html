What is Interlude ?

Interlude is a web social media app where people can share and discover music with everyone thanks to a personalized feed.

↓ See how below ↓

  1. Register
    First you have to register, otherwise you cannot access our many functionnalities !
  2. Follow
    Then follow people with similar taste so that their posts show up on your feed !
  3. Hashtags
    Another way to have posts show up on your feed is to use "#" in the music section in settings !
  4. Scroll
    Now that you have done everything, just scroll down your feed to make wonderful discoveries !

Share Music

Use Interlude to make posts, share musics thanks to Youtube or Spotify links.

As you can see on the right, you can also add a caption.

Once it's posted, people can interact with your post by liking and commenting.

Customize your profile

You have the possibility to add a profile picture and a bio like any other social media.
We added a special element called "My musical tastes" where you can write about the genres or artists you like.
And here is a reminder that if you put a "#" in this section.
All posts containing the same "#" in their caption will automatically be added to your feed !

Our team

Interlude was created as part of our end-of-year portfolio project at Holberton School Laval.

"We wanted to create a place where anyone can discuss, discover and introduce music to anyone !

Building on the knowledge we had acquired in our first year, we developed Interlude."
